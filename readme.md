# Lead Quality Scorer

## What It Does
Automatically scores customer inquiries on a 1-10 scale to help sales teams prioritize hot leads.

## Features
- ✅ Intelligent lead scoring (1-10 scale)
- ✅ Identifies hot leads automatically
- ✅ Explains reasoning for each score
- ✅ Batch processing (score 100+ leads at once)
- ✅ JSON output (CRM-ready format)
- ✅ Analytics dashboard (summary stats)
- ✅ Hot lead extraction (fast follow-up)

## Scoring Scale
- **1-3:** Not a lead (spam, no interest, no budget)
- **4-6:** Potential (curious, uncertain fit, low urgency)
- **7-10:** Hot lead (ready to buy, clear need, has budget, urgency)

## Scoring Factors
✓ Budget available (can they afford it?)
✓ Timeline/urgency (when do they need it?)
✓ Problem fit (does your product solve their problem?)
✓ Decision authority (are they authorized to buy?)
✓ Company legitimacy (is this a real business?)
✓ Urgency signals (emergency, crisis, deadline?)
